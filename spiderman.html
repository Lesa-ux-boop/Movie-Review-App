Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 (August 1962).

Spider-Man has been adapted into various media, including animated series, live-action films, and video games.

Some popular Spider-Man films include Sam Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy, The Amazing Spider-Man series, and the recent Spider-Man: Homecoming and Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.

Spider-Man has also appeared in various animated series, including the 1994 Spider-Man: The Animated Series and the more recent Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.
