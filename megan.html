Megan

Megan is a science fiction horror film released in 2022, directed by Gerard Johnstone. The film follows a lifelike doll named Megan, designed to be a child's greatest companion, who becomes self-aware and overprotective.

The story explores themes of artificial intelligence, obsession, and the consequences of playing God.

Megan received positive reviews for its blend of horror and humor, as well as its commentary on technology and parenting.

The film's success has led to discussions about potential sequels and the future of AI in society.
