Moana

Moana is an animated musical adventure film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios, released in 2016. The film features the voice of Auli'i Cravalho as Moana, a spirited Polynesian teenager who sets sail on a daring mission to save her island.

Accompanied by the demigod Maui, voiced by Dwayne Johnson, Moana embarks on a journey across the ocean to restore the heart of Te Fiti, the goddess of creation.

Moana received critical acclaim for its animation, music, and cultural representation, becoming a beloved film for audiences of all ages.

The film's soundtrack, featuring songs by Lin-Manuel Miranda, includes the hit song "How Far I'll Go."
