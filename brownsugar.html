Brown Sugar

Brown Sugar is a romantic drama film directed by Rick Famuyiwa, released in 2002. The film stars Taye Diggs and Sanaa Lathan as childhood friends who share a love for hip-hop music.

The story follows their journey as they navigate their careers in the music industry while exploring their feelings for each other.

Brown Sugar received positive reviews for its authentic portrayal of hip-hop culture and its engaging storyline.

The film's soundtrack features a mix of classic and contemporary hip-hop tracks, adding to its overall appeal.
