The Avengers

The Avengers is a superhero film released in 2012, directed by Joss Whedon. The film features a team of superheroes from the Marvel Comics universe, including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye.

The story follows the formation of the Avengers as they come together to stop the villain Loki from invading Earth.

The Avengers received critical acclaim for its ensemble cast, action sequences, and humor, becoming a major box office success.

The film's success led to the creation of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, with several sequels and spin-offs featuring the Avengers characters.
