Catwoman

Catwoman is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by writer Bill Finger and artist Bob Kane.

Catwoman has been adapted into various media, including animated series, live-action films, and video games.

Some popular Catwoman films include her appearances in the Batman film series, such as Batman Returns and The Dark Knight Rises.

Catwoman has also appeared in various animated series, including Batman: The Animated Series and Gotham Girls.
