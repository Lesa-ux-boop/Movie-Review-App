Suicide Squad

Suicide Squad is a superhero film released in 2016, directed by David Ayer. The film features a team of antiheroes from the DC Comics universe, including Harley Quinn, Deadshot, and the Joker.

The story follows the formation of the Suicide Squad, a group of incarcerated villains who are recruited by the government to carry out dangerous missions in exchange for reduced sentences.

Suicide Squad received mixed reviews from critics but was praised for its performances, particularly Margot Robbie as Harley Quinn.

The film's soundtrack, featuring a mix of popular songs, became a commercial success.
