The Incredibles

The Incredibles is an animated superhero film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures in 2004. The film follows the Parr family, a family of superheroes forced to live undercover after their powers are outlawed.

When a new villain emerges, Mr. Incredible and his family must come out of hiding to save the day.

The Incredibles received critical acclaim for its animation, storytelling, and themes of family and identity.

The film won two Academy Awards and has become a beloved classic for audiences of all ages.
