Black Widow

Black Widow is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was created by writer Stan Lee, scripter Larry Lieber, and artist Don Heck.

Black Widow has been adapted into various media, including animated series, live-action films, and video games.

Some popular Black Widow films include her appearances in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, such as Iron Man 2, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, and her standalone film Black Widow.

Black Widow has also appeared in various animated series, including The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes and Marvel's What If...?
